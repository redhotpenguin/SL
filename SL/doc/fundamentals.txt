The five fundamental questions.

1) What they're going to

Develop and sell an ad serving firewall to businesses as a way of offsetting
the costs of providing internet access.

2) Why users need it

Businesses who provide internet access need to offset the operational costs of
and generate revenue from the internet access.

People who need internet access want it for free

3) How large the market is

Potentially every business that provides internet access

4) Who the competitors are

Vendors which provide the businesses with paid internet access gateways.
Free wireless organizations who seek to provide internet access for no cost.

5) Why this company is going to beat them

This company will be first to market with this solution, and patent the ad
serving firewall
